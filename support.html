Become a patron

Patrons provide regular, recurring funding so that I can focus on doing the work that I love instead of spending that time hunting through job boards, polishing my resume and sitting in interviews. At the moment, I have accounts with two crowd-sourcing services that support the patron model:

Gittip pays out pledges weekly. You can pledge as little as 1¢, or as much as $100, per week. Learn more.

The plan I've chosen on Patreon pays out pledges monthly. Patreon's system also lets creators specify pledge levels that unlock bonus material, access, etc.. For more information, see my profile.

Commission work

Bounties are rewards or incentives that the an individual or organization offers in return for a bug being fixed or feature implemented. I try to keep abreast of bounties being offered that relate to my skills, experience and/or interests. If you've filed a bounty that you would like me to consider, feel free to let me know about it.
